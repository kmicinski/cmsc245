Piazza

Course discussions will happen primarily on Piazza. Piazza is an online discussion forum that allows students to post and answer each other’s questions. Questions can be marked as private (only to be seen by faculty) and/or anonymous (even the professors won’t know who you are). Note that participation on Piazza (particularly posting engaging and helpful feedback on other students’ questions and work) will factor into course participation.

Link to our piazza site. Note that you will need to setup an account (using your @haverford|brynmawr|swarthmore.edu addredss) if you do not already have one.
